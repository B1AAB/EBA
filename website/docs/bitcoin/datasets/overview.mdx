To help you get started quickly, 
we offer several entry points into the dataset. 
Choose the path that best matches your application 
and available resources.



* Skip all data pre-processing & **start modeling**

    - [Download sampled communities](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vjalili/bitcoin-graph-sampled-communities)


* Skip the ETL pipeline & **sample your own graphs**
    - [Download Neo4j Database Dump](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1bAsjgVaIQrG2TDGkMtIEDPX8xKoiHJUf?usp=sharing).
    - [Documentation](/docs/bitcoin/etl/import#load) on importing the dataset into Neo4j.
    - [Documentation](/docs/bitcoin/sampling/overview) on sampling your own graphs.


* Skip the Bitcoin node sync and chain traverse & **use summary statistics**
    - [Download](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1_V-PNh9yhWDzQyvYj4If3m2HxNF6wiuz/view?usp=sharing) block summary statistics, & check [documentation](./stats).
